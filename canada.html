With innovation happening every day, it is important to return to our roots every once and awhile.
From 2017, our founder was a Canadian. So we would like to show the world just how much Canada has helped us, and space exploration grow not only with an outstanding founder.

Canada took part in some of the earliest space travel innovations, such as voyager 2. Its mission was to explore planets unreachable (in their age), so that humans might be able to understand these titans of celestial objects. Canada jumped on the opportunity to help, and contributed in the development of telemetry instruments for voyager 2. Young Calder White was inspired by the images of jupiter, and its many moons. Little did he know, he would start cruise64 ten years later.
